RefurbyGo

A Direct-to-Consumer brand redefining trust in India’s refurbished electronics market through premium quality, lean sourcing, and strong unit economics.

Our Goal

To build India’s most trusted D2C platform for premium refurbished smartphones and laptops, delivering a new-product experience at better prices while maintaining sustainable margins.

Market Opportunity

Large Market

India’s refurbished smartphone market is valued in billions and growing rapidly due to rising device costs and shorter upgrade cycles.

Clear Gap

Consumers lack trust due to fake parts, poor quality checks, and weak after-sales support.

D2C Advantage

Eliminating marketplaces allows better pricing, higher margins, and direct customer trust.

Strategy & Competitive Edge

32-point quality diagnostics with strict refurbishment standards
Bulk sourcing at 40–60% below retail prices
100% prepaid model ensuring predictable cash flow
Early break-even and fast inventory rotation
